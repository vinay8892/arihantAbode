Arihant Abode Overview

 

Welcome to Arihant Group's new project. We are delighted to introduce our upcoming residential endeavor following the successful completion of the Arihant Arden and Arihant Ambar projects in Sector 10, Noida Extension, Greater Noida West. ARIHANT ABODE has been officially granted the Rera Number up-rera.in - UPRERAPRJ15792.

The mission of ARIHANT ABODE is to provide one of the finest lifestyles, creating a notable landmark within your community. Strategically situated in Sector 10, Noida Extension, Greater Noida West, ARIHANT ABODE boasts excellent connectivity to Noida, Noida Expressway, FNG Expressway, and NH 24 Ghaziabad. .
Location Advantage: The location of Arihant Abode is well suited for the ones who want to enjoy a contemporary lifestyle with all the nearby major utilities like schools, colleges, hospitals,supermarts, parks and recreational centers.

Arihant Abode allows you to experience the most precious feeling a home!
